About

Loima Students Association (LSA) was formed to be a platform and a voice through which the students could air their views as well as make their Contributions to the general issues affecting the community and students’ body in various institutions of learning in the Country.

Loima Objectives

The world today relies on its youthful population for socio-economic empowerment. That is, through purposed groupings such as Loima Students Association (LSA), it is easier to challenge the status quo in provision of welfare services, right gender issues, create mutual political relations in order to give the youth a transformational voice in matters of community concern

  • To create opportunities for leadership development, learning, student engagement and fostering of shared interest;
  • To develop positive relationship with the general public through participation in community service activities;
  • To increase collaboration with non-political organizations, Professional Associations and other groups that share the aims and objectives of LSA;
  • Rolling out mentorship programs and Lobbying for internships, industrial attachments and job placement opportunities;
  • Creating and looking out for mutual partnerships with NGOs, CSOs, individuals and the Governments;
  • To support students’ personal development through formal training sessions, interactions through community social responsibilities, self-realization and interpersonal cooperation.

This intervention with favourable legal environment and stakeholder support will immensely minimize the pangs of school deferments, dropouts and misguided academic purpose through frequent mentorships and coaching programs for students and pupils in the various schools spread throughout Loima sub-county.
